In a few weeks I’ll turn fifty. Today I completed a 50 km hiking challenge. These two “fifties” resonate quite nicely together. Maybe it’s a bit sentimental...

But hey — fifty is the new thirty, right? Just look at Eszter and me (even though she’s younger than I am): we’re in great physical and mental shape. Age is just a number — it’s exactly as much as you decide to attach to it. I’m training for 100. In hiking, and in years too.
