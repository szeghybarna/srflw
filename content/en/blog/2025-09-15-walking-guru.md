I’ve decided to write a book in which I share the 6M Method I developed.

What is the 6M Method?

The 6M Method is built on six pillars. You don’t have to change everything at once — taking the first step is enough.

1. Measure:
finding balance in calories and awareness

2. Mobility:
the joy of moving freely

3. Motor:
strengthening your heart and endurance

4. Mental:
sleep, stress management, emotional balance

5. Movement:
redefining what an active life means

6. Menu:
the connection between food and quality of life

What does RESTART give you?

- Simple, easy-to-follow steps
- Science-based yet understandable methods
- Guidance rooted in personal experience
- Emotionally supportive reflections
- The beginning of a sustainable, joyful life

The book is expected to be released in 2026.
